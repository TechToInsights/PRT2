Hello, I’m Ahtesham Hashmi, a data enthusiast and a lifelong learner who thrives at the intersection of technology, analytics, and leadership.

Growing up in the vibrant city of Indore, I’ve always been curious about how things work and how innovation can drive meaningful change. That curiosity has shaped my journey from engineering to data analytics – a journey fueled by a passion for solving problems and uncovering insights.

But technical expertise alone wasn’t enough—I wanted to use data to uncover deeper insights and drive real-world impact. My passion for analysis goes beyond just numbers; I see patterns everywhere, from business operations to everyday decisions. To fuel this curiosity, I pursued an MBA in Business Analytics, equipping myself with advanced skills in data analysis, visualization, and business intelligence. Additionally, I have hands-on experience as a Data Analyst and Corporate Trainer, delivering insights that enhance decision-making. During my tenure at Codenscious Technologies, I played a key role in optimizing business processes, and my contributions were recognized as I successfully led analytics projects that improved efficiency and performance.

And my learning never stops. Beyond my professional experience, I’ve tackled numerous personal projects(50+), applying my skills in SQL, Python, Power BI, Excel, and Tableau. From integrating vast datasets with millions of records to conducting deep exploratory analysis, web scraping, sentiment analysis, and optimizing customer engagement strategies, I’ve consistently challenged myself to extract meaningful insights and build dynamic, visually compelling dashboards.

Data isn’t just about numbers—it’s about storytelling, clarity, and impact. I believe in the power of effective communication, ensuring that complex insights are not only discovered but also understood and acted upon. As a corporate trainer, I have had the opportunity to deliver sessions to professionals at organizations like Indian Oil Corporation and Deloitte, where I was recognized for my ability to break down intricate data concepts into clear, actionable strategies. Presenting analytical findings in a way that resonates with both technical and non-technical audiences is something I take pride in, as it transforms raw data into meaningful decisions.

But my interest in data goes beyond analysis—it extends to the business world, where numbers drive decisions, strategies, and growth. Just as data reveals hidden patterns, businesses tell stories through their performance, and I find immense satisfaction in uncovering these narratives. My exposure to business analytics has deepened my appreciation for how companies operate, leading me to explore and simplify complex business concepts.

So, if you're looking for a data analyst or a business analyst who can crunch numbers, see the big picture, think about your business and tell a story with them, let's connect!
